Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a grassroots meet-up for builders and researchers. It has since grown into a global conference featuring cross-disciplinary tracks in AI, web, cloud, and cybersecurity.

Our Mission

We empower creators, share practical knowledge, and spark ethical innovation. Our guiding principles are openness, accessibility, and impact.
